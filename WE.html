Women Empowerment - Development with equity



Women empowerment for rights and entitlements:Unless women are associated and empowered food and social security schemes run by the govt, these would not be effective. With this understanding THD empowers, educates and sensitizes the women, extends required handholding support to the village level institutions and federations to work for their rights and entitlements through a collective and organized manner. The village level newly elected PRI representatives, govt line depts. and networking with likeminded institutions provides greater leverage to the women groups to fight for their rights. The six important schemes MGNREGS, MDM, ICDS, JSY, Pension and Food Security schemes are given priority across the operational areas. The SHGs in every village are empowered and facilitated to identify eligible beneficiaries, help them incorporate into the scheme and monitor the scheme’s effective implementation.
