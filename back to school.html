Back to School Programme:



Back to School Programme is an initiative to bring those children back to schools, who have dropped out. Despite the fact that Right to Education Act is in force, a good number of children of school going age are out of school with most of the children having dropped out at various stages of education. As part of our efforts to see all the children of school going age in schools, THDendeavours to create conducive environment in the villages as well as schools to promote children’s education with particular focus on girl child and remove barriers like lack of parental motivation and support in children’s education, non-functional and ineffective school Management committees, negative social attitudes towards girl child education etc. THD organizes various events like village meetings involving parents, youths, SHG members, meetings with dropout students and members of school Management Committees to sensitise and aware them on the importance of education.

Besides, counseling services are provided to parents of dropped out children to motivate and guide them for re-enrollment and to mainstream the children. Door to door visits are also made, apart from organizing rallies and padayatras. In order to enhance community ownership and participation for improved functioning of schools THD conducts capacity building training programs for members of School Management Committee (SMC).

The following Activities are being taken up
• Sensitisation and awareness meetings at the village level
• Rallies, demonstrations, street plays to convey the message widely
• Door to door campaigns
• Meeting and counselling for dropout children and their parents
• Sensitisation and awareness camps on Child rights
• Yoga training camps
• Training and capacity building programs for youth volunteers

Key Achievements
567 dropped out children are back in schools.
Change the behavior of parents to sending school to their children’s and realized the future of children’s as well as family education.
65 School management committees(SMC) are able to actively manage theirschools.
