About Us

THD believes in holistic community development – the concept of development purely based on equity. Since inception THD emphasises on women empowerment through mobilising women inro forming women SHGs and their apex level bodies at Gram Panchayat level.

With the implementation of Odisha Livelihood Mission, the scope of these institutions has further expanded. It plans to function as a single window source of credit and technology dissemination to improve rural livelihoods. These institutions not only function as a thrift and credit society, rather they proactively participate in community development activities as a forum.
